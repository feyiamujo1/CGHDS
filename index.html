Our Social Impact

The Redeemer's University Centre for Gender, Humanitarian, and Development Studies (CGHDS) aligns with the University's vision of producing graduates who are well-prepared to tackle current social, economic, and environmental issues through advanced research in science, technology, and humanities, driven by Information and Communication Technology (ICT). Our impact revolves around:

  • Designing policies and programs for gender, humanitarian, conflict resolution, peace-building, and sustainable development.
  • Developing social and economic guidelines for poverty reduction and pro-poor development.
  • Engaging in research, consultancies, and addressing socio-economic, political, and gender-related issues.
  • Equipping students and professionals with analytical skills for understanding gender and development.
  • Incorporating gender frameworks into curricula and administrative systems.
