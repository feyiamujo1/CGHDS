Short Courses and Programmes

Expand your knowledge and expertise through our dynamic Short Courses at the Redeemer’s University Centre for Gender, Humanitarian, and Development Studies (CGHDS). Designed to cater to diverse interests and time constraints, our short courses offer focused and intensive learning experiences in areas such as crises and disaster management, humanitarian logistics, gender policy and planning, and more. The short courses we offer are:

  • Graduate Certificate Course in Crises and Disaster Management
  • Graduate Certificate Course in Humanitarian Logistics
  • Graduate Certificate Course in Gender, Policy, and Planning for Development
  • Non-Degree Certificate Courses in Gender and Development Studies
  • Gender and Development (3 Months)
  • Gender and Health Studies (3 Months)
  • Gender, Human Rights and Access to Justice (3 Months)
  • Gender, Adolescent Sexuality and Youth Development (3 Months)
  • Women and Gender Studies (3 Months)
  • Gender and Politics
  • Women’s Political Participation and Development
